Qualitative Research Site Selection Strategy

Objective: Identify distinct demographic profiles across 81 cities to select diverse locations for qualitative fieldwork.

Data Sources: Average Household Size, Median Age, Marital Status (Single/Married/Divorced). Dependency Ratio excluded as requested.

Identified Research Profiles

Profile 1: Higher Divorce Rate

Target Demographic: - Household Structure: Standard/Small - Age Cohort: Middle Aged - Marital Dynamics: 65.5% Married, 3.6% Divorced

Recommended Fieldwork Sites (Representative): - Ankara - Antalya - Aydın

Profile 2: Large Families & Young Population & High Single Rate

Target Demographic: - Household Structure: Large (>3.5) - Age Cohort: Young (<30) - Marital Dynamics: 55.2% Married, 0.5% Divorced

Recommended Fieldwork Sites (Representative): - Diyarbakır - Muş - Mardin

Profile 3: Mature Population

Target Demographic: - Household Structure: Standard/Small - Age Cohort: Mature (>38) - Marital Dynamics: 67.4% Married, 2.2% Divorced

Recommended Fieldwork Sites (Representative): - Bolu - Isparta - Manisa
